Executive Summary

S.M Kabine General Construction was established in 2003 from humble beginnings, offering general construction and related services.

21 years later, in a very tough and demanding market, through sheer resilience, attention to detail, quality workmanship and competitive pricing, the Company has grown exponentially and today provides general maintenance and construction services to a growing client base that includes:

  • Union Buildings
  • Agricultural Research Council
  • South African Bank Note Company
  • South African Reserve Bank
  • Onderstepoort Biological Products

Shadrack Kabine is the proud owner and founder of the business with over 30 years of experience in the construction industry. He has a wealth of knowledge and is well known within the industry for his integrity and result driven ethos. His hands-on approach, exemplary leadership skills and quality workmanship makes SM Kabine general construction a much sought after partner within the industry.

S.M Kabine General Construction is a leader in providing value-added construction services to our customers by creating a successful partnership with them throughout the construction process. Our pledge is to establish lasting relationships with our customers by exceeding their expectations and gaining their trust through exceptional performance by every member of the construction team.
